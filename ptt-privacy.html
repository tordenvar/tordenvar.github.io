Privacy Policy

Below is the privacy policy that dictates your use of the iOS application "Pass The Trivia" created by Leif Johanson ("The Developer"). Pass The Trivia is a pass and play trivia game which can be played by 1-6 players.

What information does Pass The Trivia obtain and how does Pass The Trivia use said information?

Information Provided by the User

Pass The Trivia obtains the information provided when you download Pass The Trivia from the App Store. When Pass The Trivia is being used, each user generally provides a) their in game (which may or may not be their legal name); b) transaction-related information, for example when the user downloads other applications created by The Developer; c) information provided in e-mails sent to tordenvarapps@gmail.com for support.

Automatically Collected Information

The Developer does not collect nor store any user information. Pass The Trivia was built with Apple's Xcode SDK, refer to their privacy policy for more detailed information. Pass the Trivia does not use any third party advertising, therefore no personal data is collected for advertising.

Real Time Location

Pass The Trivia nor The Developer does not collect any location information of the user's device.

Third Party Access

Pass The Trivia nor The Developer does not transmit any personal information of any user. Information is only shared with third parties as described in this privacy policy.

Advertising Data Collection

Pass The Trivia, currently (v1.0), does not use advertisements, therefore does not collect advertising data.

User Management to Their Data

Data that was provided by the user (see the "User Provided Information" section) can be deleted at request by e-mailing tordenvarapps@gmail.com.

Security

We do our best to safeguard the confidentiality of your information. We do not disclose any of your information to any third party, as defined in this privacy policy. Please keep in mind that security breaches are still possible despite safeguards.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will be updated occasionally for any reason determined by The Developer. We will notify the user of these changes by posting the new privacy policy on this website. It is recommended the user checks back regularly for any changes, as continued use of Pass The Trivia is deemed approval of all changes.

Consent

In order to conform to the General Data Protection Regulation, we will not process any personal data. Due to this, we are not required to receive your consent, as use of Pass The Trivia is deemed consent.

Contact

If the user has any questions about their privacy while using Pass The Trivia, they should contact us via e-mail at tordenvarapps@gmail.com.
