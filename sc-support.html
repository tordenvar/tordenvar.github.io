FAQ

How many levels are there?

There are currently 1500 levels across 10 different stages! Spell Champ is continuously updated, so check back for updates!

Are there duplicate words?

To the best of our knowledge, each stage has a unique word. Some stages have different forms of a word (i.e. separate and separately), but there should be no exact duplicates. If there are, please let us know at tordenvarapps@gmail.com!

There is a word spelled incorrectly. What can I do?

Keep in mind every word is spelled in the American-English variation (as opposed to British-English). If a word is misspelled, please shoot us an email at tordenvarapps@gmail.com and we will do our very best to fix it ASAP.

I have a business inquiry and I would love to get in contact, how can I do that?

Please email us at tordenvarapps@gmail.com for all business inquiries.

There is a bug and the game is not functioning correctly.

Please email us at tordenvarapps@gmail.com! The app is currently in its first version so bugs may happen, even after lengthy vigorous testing.

Questions? Comments? Concerns? Suggestions?

Please shoot us an email at tordenvarapps@gmail.com. We are a very small development team, so feedback is greatly appreciated, and if you love Pass The Trivia, please give us 5 stars on the App Store! Thanks!
